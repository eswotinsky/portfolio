    About Me

    I'm fascinated by languages - both natural and technical. I hold an MA in Linguistics from the University of Amsterdam, and a BA in Linguistics and Communication (double major) from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

    As a developer, I'm driven in large part by my love for puzzles. It's a genuine thrill to explore new ways of thinking every day, and to find creative solutions to tricky problems.

    In addition to web development, I occasionally write web copy and blogs - both professionally and personally. You can follow my own blog, where I post about topics like entrepreneurship, writing, and travel, at ericswotinsky.com.

    When I'm not creating things, I love to explore the world. So far, I've been to 22 countries across 4 continents, and am aiming to visit at least 100 nations within my lifetime.
