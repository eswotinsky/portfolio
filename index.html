    About Me

    I'm fascinated by languages - both natural and technical. I hold an MA in Linguistics from the University of Amsterdam, and a BA in Linguistics and Communication (double major) from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

    As a developer, I'm driven in large part by my love for puzzles and games. It's a genuine thrill to explore new ways of thinking every day, and to find creative solutions to tricky problems. I'm currently attending Epicodus in downtown Seattle, where I'm developing my programming skills full-time alongside other up-and-coming developers.

    In addition to web development, I sometimes write web copy and blogs - both professionally and personally. You can follow my own blog, where I post about topics like entrepreneurship, writing, and travel, at ericswotinsky.com.
